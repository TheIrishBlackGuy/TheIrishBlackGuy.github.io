The 404 bot

This character is a result of a prompt reading to creating a display to be shown when a web browser cannot be located a.k.a a 404 Error page. When thinking of the idea of a '404 Error', my first thought went towards something mechanical smoking from steam, crunching to a loose gear or dented in a way it no longer works. Putting these attributes together, I starting to think of robots breaking in media like in the tv show Futurama or the movie I, Robot, A robot is something familiar to people that can be broken through a technical error. So following that thought, I designed a robot utilizing simple shapes because people don't need a lot of information thrown at them to know a page can't be reached. The second reason I chose the simple shapes method is because I like to show that you don't need to master an arts class in order to produce something cool from basic tools.
