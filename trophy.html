The Gaming Trophies

I have always had a fascination with 3d modelling and had always wanted to dive into the world of Blender. I had experience with simpler 3d modeling software like Blockbench and BrickLink so I had an understanding of navigation. As a starter project, I wanted to create something I was familiar with. Since most 3D models I had seen online in my corner of the internet were local to videogames, So using basic shapes and principles, I tried produce some recognizable objects and items. As I evolve through my work with 3d modeling, I want to create more detailed objects and step into character design. But at this stage, I'm proud that I made this on my first attempt.
