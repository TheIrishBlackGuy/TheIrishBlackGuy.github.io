My 3D Avatar

When I was a young teen, I got really invested in pixel art. I made a bunch of designs and imitated pokemon sprites and other pixel aesthetic characters. Gradually, I got better and began creating some small things of my own. After a while, I eventually create a 2d pixel avatar of myself and I thought he was awesome. Simple but still recognizable as me. I did always want him to be more but adding more pixels or increasing his resolution just didn't work for me. When I had begun to use blender, it finally clicked where he was going to be heading next. I not have a 3D model of my avatar thats opens whole new doors of where I can go with him and I look forward to his future endeavors.
