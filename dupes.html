My Dupes and I

Prior to making this video, I had operated cameras, edited some audio and visual in the past but, I had never messed with editing the footage itself before. Once I had learned how masking worked, I knew how I wanted to use it. I have always spoke of how I would love a second me to talk to or interact with. Though I can't have that in reality, I could now simulate it digitally. I loved scripting and choreographing this with myself though. It was difficult in some areas where I overlap but trying my best I think I did a good job for my first masking attempt.
