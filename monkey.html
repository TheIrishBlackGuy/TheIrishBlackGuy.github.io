De Monkays

This is a piece I made for fun as my first introduction to Photoshop but I still find it funny today. I like monkeys, the animals, and I like Gorillaz, the band. I just thought what if they were one and the same. So I made a digital collage to produce my thought. I had a lot of fun making this, giggling as it came together, realizing how stupid it looked. But that doesn't matter. You can find and make stupid anywhere and others question it, so what? They just don't think like you. Not everyone is required to like your art. But if they do, cool. They see a part of you others don't.
