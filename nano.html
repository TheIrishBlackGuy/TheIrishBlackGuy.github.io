The Nano Gaunlet

Don't take any offense looking at this. I'm sure you're a nice person.
I remember building this, learning the fingers were positionable and I knew it would be in this position for as long as I owned it. So when I was drawing it in Illustrator, I didn't want to drop my humor. I chose to draw this toy as a still life project as I see myself as a playful person and I never want to let that go. Any opportunity I can plug a passion of mine into a project, I will always do so because as much as I like to showcase my work, I like to show people a part of myself and let others see the artist beyond the work. I am very happy with how sharp and distinguishable the hard edges of the LEGO pieces turned out and really enjoyed the process of this piece.
